About Us

Welcome to Rapt, a community-driven initiative dedicated to supporting and uplifting our neighbors through the provision of nutritious food and essential resources. At Rapt, we believe that everyone deserves access to healthy meals and the necessary tools for a better quality of life.

Initiatives

Special Supplemental Nutrition Program for Women, Infants, and Children (WIC)

WIC is an essential program for supporting the health and well-being of vulnerable populations, ensuring that women, infants, and children have access to the nutrition they need during critical stages of development.

Learn More

Anganwadi Workers

The important services provided by the Anganwadi workers are- primary health checkup and immunization, supplementary nutrition, educating people (in particular women) about family planning, their health, and well-being.

Learn More

Ministry of Health & Family Welfare

Initially the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare had two Departments, Each of these Departments was headed by the Secretary to the Government of India:-Department of Health & Family Welfare and Department of Health Research.

Integrated Child Development Service Programme (ICDS)

This is a unique programme under which a package of integrated services consisting of supplementary nutrition, immunization, health check up, referal and education service are provided to the most vulnerable groups even within children and women
